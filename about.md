# Toby J. Protsman
**Founder & CEO, Broken Ladder Recovery Systems Inc.**

With over 25 years of experience in high-stakes manufacturing, construction, and technical leadership, Toby J. Protsman brings a unique, safety-first perspective to the field of recovery technology. 

### The Vision
The "Broken Ladder" philosophy is built on the belief that accountability should not come at the cost of human dignity. After years of observing the limitations of traditional, invasive monitoring, Toby founded Broken Ladder to bridge the gap between **biometric precision** and **compassionate care**.

### Our Commitment
> "We aren't just building a wearable; we are building a foundation of trust. By providing objective, real-time data through the Amulet ring and the Vigil platform, we empower individuals to prove their commitment to wellness while giving providers the tools they need to intervene before a crisis occurs."

### Background & Expertise
* **Safety & Leadership:** 25+ years in manufacturing and construction safety.
* **Academic Excellence:** 3.8 GPA in Business Management & Nonprofit Studies.
