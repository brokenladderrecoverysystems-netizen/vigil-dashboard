# Joint Venture Agreement (Master Template)
**Strategic Collaboration: Broken Ladder Recovery Systems Inc. & [Partner Name]**

### 1. Purpose
This agreement establishes a collaborative partnership to integrate clinical recovery expertise with the **Amulet/Vigil** technology suite.

### 2. Equity and Ownership (Cross-Equity Swap)
* **Partner Interest:** [Partner Organization] will hold **20% ownership** in Broken Ladder Recovery Systems Inc.
* **Reciprocal Interest:** Broken Ladder Recovery Systems Inc. will hold **20% ownership** in [Partner Organization].

### 3. Investment Contributions
* **Clinical Partner:** Financial resources, personnel, and recovery expertise.
* **Broken Ladder:** Hardware (Amulet), AI Platform (Vigil), and data resources.

### 4. Profit Sharing
Profits will be shared proportional to equity ownership, aligning the financial success of the technology with the clinical outcomes of the facility.

### 5. Intellectual Property
New IP developed during this collaboration will be co-owned, ensuring both parties benefit from future platform scaling.

### 6. Managerial Role
Broken Ladder will lead technical project areas and participate in strategic decision-making to ensure data integrity and system security.
